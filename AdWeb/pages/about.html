Ashwinkumar & Co. is an established & trusted name in the field of Metrology. Since, 1976, we have been serving the manufacturing industry with high quality precision instruments for their various measurement and quality control needs.

Our offerings are focused on mechanical & dimensional gauging products such as micrometers, calipers, dial indicators, gauge blocks, height gauges, bore gauges, surface roughness testers and various other measuring instruments.

We cater to the needs of diverse manufacturing companies from sectors like automobile, forging, casting, heavy engineering, power generation, aircraft, railways and many more.

We are Authorized Distributors for Mitutoyo make precision measuring instruments in India. MITUTOYO founded in 1934, is a Japanese multinational corporation specializing in measuring instruments and metrological technology.
